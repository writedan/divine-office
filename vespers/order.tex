\vesectionp{Introduction}\label{vespers:introduction}

	\instruct{The congregation and ministers kneel and quietly make these prayers.}
	
	In the name \red{\GreCross{17}} of the all-holy and undivided Trinity. Amen.
	
	Our Father, who art in heaven, hallowed be thy name. Thy kingdom come. Thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread and forgive us our trespasses as we forgive those who trespass against us. And lead us not into temptation but deliver us from evil.
	
	Hail Mary, full of grace, the Lord is with thee. Blessed art thou among women and blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus. Holy Mary, Mother of God, pray for us sinners now and at the hour of our death. Amen.
	
	\instruct{The congregation and ministers stand as the officiant begins.}

	\instruct{The congregation and ministers say this together. Everywhere the three Persons of the Holy Trinity are invoked together, bow and cross yourself.}

\vesectionp{Conclusion}\label{vespers:conclusion}

	\instruct{The congregation and ministers kneel as the cantors turn to the \blueribbon and sing the appointed Kyrie. Once this has been completed, make this prayer quietly.}
	
	Our Father, who art in heaven, hallowed be thy name. Thy kingdom come. Thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread and forgive us our trespasses as we forgive those who trespass against us. And lead us not into temptation but deliver us from evil.
